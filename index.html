Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about free movie streaming sites.

What are the best free streaming sites in 2026?
Based on our testing in April 2026, MoonTV, 123Cine, Dulo, NetPrime, and FlixZone consistently top the charts for reliability, video quality, and low ad-intrusiveness. MoonTV and 123Cine are particularly impressive for their large content libraries and clean interfaces.
Are free streaming sites legal?
The legality of streaming depends heavily on your country. StreamVault lists publicly accessible sites and does not host any content itself. We always recommend using a VPN for added privacy and checking your local laws. For 100% legal options, platforms like Tubi, Pluto TV, and Peacock's free tier offer licensed content at no cost.
Do I need to create an account to watch?
No — that's one of our core requirements. Every site we list allows you to start watching immediately without creating an account or providing an email address. If a site we've listed starts requiring registration, we remove it within 24 hours.
How do I block ads on streaming sites?
Install uBlock Origin on desktop (works on Firefox and Chrome) — it's the most effective free ad-blocker available. On mobile, use Brave browser which has built-in shields. Our ad scores are measured with both tools, so Green-rated sites will give you an almost ad-free experience with either tool.
Why do some streaming sites disappear?
Free streaming sites frequently receive DMCA takedown notices from copyright holders, which can force them to shut down or change their domain. We monitor every site on our list daily and update working URLs within 24 hours. Bookmarking StreamVault ensures you always have current links rather than bookmarking individual streaming sites directly.
How often is this list updated?
We check and update our rankings daily. New sites are evaluated before being added, dead links are removed, and ad scores are re-tested whenever a site's behavior changes. The "Last Updated" badge at the top of the table reflects the most recent review date.
